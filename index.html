Letter from Secretary General

Dear Delegates,

It gives me great pleasure to invite you to the third session of the ISM MUN. Our team has put their heart and soul into ensuring that ISM MUN matches up to the high standards of the conferences held all over East India.

The committees and the agendas which we have this year at this conference are sure to include something that will excite each and every delegate who attends. Whether you want to work towards framing environmental guidelines which the world would then abide by, or wish to improve global conditions by deliberating over the crucial issues regarding economic sanctions; whether you revel in the intense debate of the security council, thrive in the element of unknown held by a crisis committee, or enjoy humiliating other delegates by putting your pen to paper, as a member of the press-ISM MUN has something that you will love.

To the first-time delegates out there- ISM MUN has witnessed the making of many a great MUNer. I truly hope that you get a similar experience out of this conference. After all, there is so much you can take away from each and every MUN conference- an increase in confidence, improved oratory and debating skills, and perhaps most important of all- brilliant experiences and moments! Combine this with our annual cult fest Srijan and the spell binding Star Nights and other attractions on display, trust me this will be one of your best conferences EVER! Even if this is your first conference, I can assure you that there is nothing to be worried about. Our chairs are some of the friendliest and most approachable people you will ever meet, as you will soon find out. I hope that I've encouraged you to take the plunge into MUN, and give you this one warning-it's extremely addictive. After your first conference, you won't be able to wait until your next! Plus we will also have a delegate night which will further help you ease into the ebb and flow of a MUN and will also serve as an excellent ice breaker which WILL come in handy later on!

And of course, MUN veterans - I promise you that you'll find something unique at ISM MUN. It won't be just another conference that you've attended. The level of debate will be extraordinary, and the committees will be extremely enjoyable, interesting while at the same time being cerebral enough for you to properly exercise those grey cells and vocal chords of yours. With delegations attending from all over the country, you'll make some great friends and memories. We're also offering you the chance to become a part of the executive board, which is an opportunity I hope you'll take advantage of, to add another dimension to your MUNing careers.

To sum it all up, ISM MUN will be a unique and amazing experience, whether it's your first MUN or your umpteenth. I look forward to meeting all of you in March!

Sincerely,
Pranjal Dwivedi
ISMMUN 2014
